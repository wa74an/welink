About

Bridging the gap
for GCC students.

We Link was built to solve one of the most frustrating obstacles facing Gulf students who choose to study in the United Kingdom — the requirement for a UK based guarantor.

Every year, thousands of students from Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, the UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman arrive in the UK to study at some of the world's finest universities. Many arrive with strong financial backing and a genuine desire to build their futures here. And yet, they consistently run into the same wall: landlords and letting agents who require a UK based guarantor before they will rent to an international student.

We Link removes that wall. We act as the UK based guarantor on behalf of our students, giving landlords the assurance they need and giving students access to a wider, better quality range of housing.

Beyond the guarantor service, we maintain a curated network of verified properties in cities with large GCC student populations, including Newcastle, Manchester, London, Birmingham, and Edinburgh. Every listing on our platform is hand verified by our team before it is published.
