3. Eligibility

To use our guarantor service as a Student, you must:

  • Be a national of Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, the UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, or Oman
  • Be enrolled at or have a confirmed offer from a recognised UK higher education institution
  • Be 18 years of age or older
  • Provide accurate and complete information during the application process

We reserve the right to decline any application at our sole discretion, including where we are not satisfied with the information provided.

4. The guarantor service

4.1 What we provide. Where We Link approves your application, we will issue a Guarantor Letter confirming our commitment to guarantee your tenancy obligations for the duration agreed with your Landlord. This letter is addressed to your Landlord or letting agent.

4.2 Scope of the guarantee. Our guarantee covers unpaid rent and damage beyond fair wear and tear, up to a maximum of [two months' rent / as specified in the Guarantor Letter]. The precise scope will be set out in the Guarantor Letter issued to your Landlord.

4.3 Your obligations. In accepting the guarantor service, you agree to:

  • Pay your rent on time and in full for the duration of the tenancy
  • Notify We Link immediately if you anticipate being unable to meet a rent payment
  • Not sublet the property without written consent from your Landlord and We Link
  • Maintain the property in good condition throughout the tenancy

4.4 Recovery of costs. If We Link is required to make any payment to a Landlord under a guarantee, you agree to repay those amounts to We Link in full. We reserve the right to pursue recovery through appropriate legal channels.

We Link maintains a curated selection of verified property listings. While we make reasonable efforts to ensure the accuracy of listing information, we do not guarantee the availability, condition, or suitability of any listed property. Tenancy agreements are entered into directly between Students and Landlords.

We Link is not a party to the tenancy agreement itself, except in our capacity as guarantor where a Guarantor Letter has been issued.

6. Landlord terms

By listing a property on the We Link platform, you confirm that:

  • You are the owner of the property or are duly authorised to let it
  • The property meets all legal requirements for residential letting in England and Wales (or Scotland or Wales as applicable), including applicable safety standards
  • All information provided about the property is accurate and up to date

Where a tenancy is completed via the We Link platform, a commission of one month's rent is payable to We Link within 14 days of the tenancy start date. We Link will issue an invoice for this amount.

7. Fees and payment

Students are charged an annual guarantor service fee based on their monthly rent, paid once per year. The applicable fee will be communicated to you during the application process and confirmed before any commitment is made. Landlords are charged a one month rent commission on each successful placement, as described in Section 6. All fees are exclusive of VAT where applicable.

We reserve the right to amend our fee structure with reasonable notice. Any changes will not affect placements already in progress.
