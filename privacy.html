Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Version 1.0 (Draft)

1. Who we are

We Link is a Kuwaiti company (Company Registration: [TBD]) providing guarantor and housing services for GCC students in the United Kingdom. We are committed to handling your personal data in line with UK data protection standards.

For all data protection enquiries, contact us at: privacy@welink-uk.com.

2. What this policy covers

This policy explains how We Link collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

It applies to all individuals who interact with our platform, including prospective and current students, landlords, and website visitors.

5. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with the following parties where necessary:

  • Supabase Inc. — our database and authentication provider (data hosted in the EU)
  • Resend Inc. — our transactional email provider
  • Landlords and letting agents — limited identity and guarantor information required to complete a tenancy agreement
  • Legal and regulatory authorities — where required by law

All third party processors are subject to data processing agreements and are required to handle your data in accordance with UK GDPR.

6. International transfers

Some of our service providers process data outside the UK. Where data is transferred internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions recognised under UK law.

7. How long we keep your data

  • Application and tenancy records: 7 years from the end of the relevant tenancy or relationship
  • Identity documents: deleted or returned within 12 months of the end of the tenancy, unless a longer retention period is required by law
  • Marketing preferences: until you withdraw consent
  • Website analytics data: 26 months (rolling)

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — ask us to delete your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to restrict processing — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@welink-uk.com. We will respond within one calendar month. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and to understand how our site is used. You can manage your cookie preferences via the banner displayed on your first visit. For full details, see our Cookie Policy [link — to be added].

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Identity documents are stored in a private, encrypted storage bucket with access restricted to authorised staff only.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Where changes are material, we will notify you by email or by displaying a prominent notice on our website. The current version will always be available at welink-uk.com/privacy.
